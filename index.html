Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Presence does one thing and does it well — keeping your Mac active and your Slack & Teams status light on.

Mouse Simulation

Small, subtle mouse movements at configurable intervals that keep your system active without getting in the way.

Optional Clicking

Enable simulated clicks for apps that require more than just mouse movement to prevent idle detection.

Lightweight

Minimal CPU and memory footprint. Presence runs silently in the background without draining your battery.

Menu Bar App

Lives in your menu bar, completely out of the way. Toggle on and off with a single click.

Configurable Timing

Set your preferred interval from every 5 seconds to every 5 minutes. Find the right balance for your workflow.

Privacy First

No data collection. No analytics. No network requests. Everything runs locally on your machine.
